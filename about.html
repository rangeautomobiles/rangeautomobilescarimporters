YOUR JOURNEY WITH RANGE AUTOMOBILES

1. Consultation & Budgeting

You share your preferred vehicle and budget. We provide expert suggestions and live market pricing based on current currency values and international trends.

2. Transparent Quotations

We provide a full cost breakdown and formal quotations. No hidden fees—you see exactly where every cent is spent.

3. Selection & Bidding

Once confirmed, we bid on vehicles based strictly on your preferences, keeping you involved in every step of the choice.

4. Logistics & Documentation

We assist with LC (Letter of Credit) opening and all administrative tasks. From the moment of purchase, you receive live tracking from the port of loading until it reaches Sri Lanka.

5. Clearance & Handover

We guide you through the tax clearance process so you see the true value and worth of your investment. Finally, we hand over your keys, warranty books, and a vehicle ready for the road.
